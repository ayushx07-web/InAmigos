Who We Are

A Movement Born From
Compassion & Purpose

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation started with a simple belief — that a kinder society is possible when people come together. Based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we have grown into a nationally recognized non-profit operating across Chhattisgarh, Delhi, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Madhya Pradesh.

We address the root causes of inequality: lack of education, food insecurity, exclusion of women, environmental degradation, and neglect of animals. Through structured, programmatic interventions, we create lasting change — not just one-time relief.

✅ Section 8 Company ✅ 80G & 12A Certified ✅ CSR-1 Registered ✅ NITI Aayog — NGO Darpan ✅ ISO 9001:2015
Our Initiatives

Six Projects. One Mission.

Each project targets a specific social challenge — together they form a complete ecosystem of change.

🍱
Food & Clothing
Project SEVA

Distributing food, clothing, and essentials to the underprivileged, homeless, and disaster-affected communities across our operational states.

🌟 Feeding thousands of families monthly
📚
Education
Project Bachpanshala

Providing quality education, learning resources, and joyful environments to underprivileged children — because every child deserves a bright future.

🌟 Empowering young minds with knowledge

Women Empowerment
Project UDAAN

Equipping women with skill development, livelihood creation tools, and financial independence — helping them soar beyond social barriers.

🌟 Creating women-led livelihoods

Environment
Project PRAKRITI

Planting saplings, raising environmental awareness, and driving sustainability campaigns — healing Mother Earth one tree at a time.

🌟 Thousands of saplings planted

Animal Welfare
Project JEEV

Protecting stray animals, providing food and care, and advocating for animal rights — because every life, big or small, deserves compassion.

Youth & Skills
Project VIKAS

India's largest internship & skill-training initiative under InAmigos — developing digital, professional, and leadership skills in young Indians.

Why It Matters

Every Action Creates
Ripples of Change

In a country where millions lack access to basic education, nutrition, and opportunity — InAmigos shows that grassroots action, powered by compassion, can shift the needle.

Our work reaches underserved communities who are often invisible to mainstream development. By combining on-ground volunteering with digital outreach and corporate CSR partnerships, we multiply our impact at scale.
